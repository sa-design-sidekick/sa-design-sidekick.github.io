About

Why work with me?

My feedback is direct, structured, and sharpens your instincts, designer-to-designer

For over 20 years, I've designed across print, digital, web, and accessibility projects for government, corporate, and creative clients. I'm experienced in WCAG and accessibility standards, inclusive design, and both legacy and modern platforms. My sessions provide tailored, in-depth critiques that help you refine your work, improve quality and consistency, and advance your skills. Combining gentle clarity with professional precision, you'll always get honest, structured feedback that helps you grow without feeling overwhelmed.

Whether you're improving your portfolio, refining individual pieces, or seeking professional insight, these sessions deliver actionable value every time.

My role is to help you sharpen your judgement while keeping the confidence-building, supportive space you need to progress.

My 3-part feedback framework

  1. Clarity What are you really trying to communicate?
  2. Craft Composition, typography, hierarchy
  3. Challenge The pro-level push that expands your instincts
