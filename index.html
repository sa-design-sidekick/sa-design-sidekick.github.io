About

Why work with me?

Over 20 years, I’ve designed across print, digital, web, and accessibility projects for government, corporate, and creative clients. I’m experienced in WCAG and accessibility standards, inclusive design, and both legacy and modern platforms. My sessions provide tailored, in-depth critiques that help you refine your work, improve quality and consistency, and advance your skills.

Whether you're improving your portfolio, refining individual pieces, or seeking professional insight, these sessions deliver actionable value every time.
